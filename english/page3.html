Uncreative people

There are no such people!

Unfortunately, there are people who think that they are not

creative, not talented, that they can and know how to do only

what they were thought by somebody else, to act only

according to what someone else orders them to do. Fortunately,

there are people who only think this way, who simply imagine

their inability to create, which originates in themselves. No matter

how many competent and spiritually creative people there are,

they are invited, or even more obliged to help people who

consider themselves uncreative.
