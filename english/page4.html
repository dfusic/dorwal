The production of an artistic picture out of a reproduction of a
drawing by means of a combined painting technique

"The human life lasts 28000 days on average, and none of them is

the same, every day in life is unique."

Just as every day in life is unique, in the same way every

reproduced specimen of and artistic picture can be unique. In

order to achieve this, we need the original drawing in black ink or

pencil. However, if we don't posses the original, a copy

(reproduction) in high quality, which should not be in color, will do

just as well.

Why?

Because we want to color that same drawing, therefore the

drawing must be black and white. That meanss that we are

finalizing the picture. The original or a copy of the drawing in

high quality can be reproduced (copied) in thousands of speciman

and each one of them can be colored differently. No matter how

hard you try to color the drawing identically, you can believe me

that you won't succeed.

This is the whole secret of producing an artistic picture as a

reproduction and unique examplar at the same time.
