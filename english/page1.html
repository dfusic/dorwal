Creativity

There is no uncreative person!

Every human being possesses an inborn creativity, already during

the birth the creativity is embedded into the human being - it has

been given to him.

During a person's life the creativity gets strengthened, developed

and extended, it gets more and more obvious, especially for

people who are engaged in creative activity.

Unfortunately, some people don't develop their personal

creativity, so it gets extinguished day by day, until it finnaly

compeletely vanished. Concerning the fact that creativity has

been given to the human being since birth there is no obstacles

which could prevent it from further development throughout the

human life. There are no class obstacle, which means that you

don't need to be materially rich in order to achieve progress

concerning your creativity, in order to achieve progress

however material wealth can help creativity develop because it

gets easier to carry out your creation (thought - idea)

Poorer finanicial status can also be helpful for the development of

creativity. It can in this case be strengthened and inspired by the

invention of various ways to improve out financial situation. I

stress this on purpose - the fact that creativity has been given to us

so generously does not imply that every person will develop their

own creativity to the same extent, or that they will use it at all.

Thus, some people can implement their creativity easily.

That is: invent and realize the invented, whereas some other

people simply don't succeed in inventing something and

additionally to carry it out. Such, less creative peope sometimes

need a helping hand, a stimulus, to overcome the state of un-

creativity and to prevent their creativity from getting extinguished

day by day and finally from dying out.
