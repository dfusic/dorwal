All you need for creating a reproduction as a
unique examplar

  • The original drawing in black ink or a copy of it in good quality 120g/m2
  • Wooden pastels (simple colored pencils like those chidren use will do just as well)
  • Dry pastels or colored chalk. In order to finalize the drawing in high quality, you should use only dry and soft pastels
  • Dry and soft wooden pastels (it is not necessary, but desirable)
  • A small sponge or fine pads for removing make-up
  • Fixative for pastels as a medium for fixing pastels after it has been applied on the paper. Instead of fixative for pastel you can use hairspray as well, which is quite cheaper.
